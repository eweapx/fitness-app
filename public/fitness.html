Voice Command Instructions
How to Use Voice Commands

Click the microphone icon in the navigation bar to start voice recognition, then speak your command clearly.

Activity Commands

Examples:

  • "Log an activity called Morning Jog for 30 minutes"
  • "Add a running workout for 45 minutes burning 320 calories"
  • "Log a cycling workout for 20 minutes with distance 5 miles"
  • "Add a weight training activity with 200 calories"
Nutrition Commands

Examples:

  • "Log a meal called Breakfast with 450 calories"
  • "Add lunch with 25 grams protein, 40 grams carbs, and 15 grams fat"
  • "Log dinner containing chicken, rice and vegetables"
  • "Add a snack with 200 calories"
Sleep Commands

Examples:

  • "Log sleep went to bed at 10:30 PM woke up at, 6:30 AM"
  • "Add sleep with quality 4"
  • "Log sleep with quality good and notes slept well"
  • "Add sleep from 11 PM to 7 AM with quality 5"
Habit & Steps Commands

Examples:

  • "Log a habit called smoking 5 times per day"
  • "Add a habit category drinking triggered by stress"
  • "Log steps 8500"
  • "Add 10000 steps"
